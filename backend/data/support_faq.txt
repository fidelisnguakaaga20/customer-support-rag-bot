Q: What is your refund policy?
A: You can request a refund within 7 days of delivery if the item is unused, in its original packaging, and you have proof of purchase. Refunds are not available for used items, clearance items, or gift cards. If the item is damaged or wrong, contact us within 48 hours of delivery for a replacement or refund. Refunds are processed back to the original payment method within 5–10 business days after we receive and inspect the returned item.

Q: Do you ship internationally?
A: No. We currently ship only within Nigeria. International shipping is not available at this time.

Q: How long does delivery take?
A: Delivery typically takes 1–3 business days within Lagos and 3–7 business days for other states in Nigeria. Delivery time may be longer during public holidays or high-volume periods.

Q: How can I track my order?
A: After your order is shipped, we send a tracking message (SMS/WhatsApp/email) with your tracking number and delivery updates. If you didn’t receive tracking details within 24 hours after shipping, contact support with your order number.

Q: What payment methods do you accept?
A: We accept debit/ATM cards, bank transfer, and USSD where available at checkout. Cash on delivery is not available unless explicitly stated on the product page.
